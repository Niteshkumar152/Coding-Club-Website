VISION

The major objective behind the setting up of a society like this in our college is to develop the spirit of coding amongst the students of not only our college but students all over India. The idea is to conduct various online and offline events that will help in the real time competitive coding experiences! Also the idea of a peer- learning programme is thoughtful where a person perfectionist in a particular domain will help guide others and this mutual learning will result in something really fruitful.

MISSION

To inculcate and instill in students the spirit of competition and to bring laurels to our institute by securing the highest ever placements . Also to achieve a level so as to enhance the college ranking in the field of technology and placements

EVENT - KAUN BANEGA ‘CODE’PATI?

CODING CLUB conducted a coding contest on 23/24/25 July. This event was declared on a social media platform and interested candidates were asked to join the whatsapp group. We had a team of 8 members. It was a trivia. Three days brought three contests along with three winners. This contest was to check coding capability as well as the intellect of the candidate.

DAY 1

There were three rounds.

  • Round1– find correct output of given codes
  • Round2 – write code of given problems
  • Round3 – write a 1000 words essay on any 2 of the given topics.

    • Candidates were awarded marks on the basis of speed and accuracy of answer.

    No. of participants: 20

    Winner: Aditi Arora

    Prize: Barbeque Nation Voucher worth Rs.1000.

    DAY 2-

    A good coder needs to be creative. This contest was to let candidates explore the creative coder in themselves.

    Candidates were asked to bring some creativity in their code on the given few topics.

    Candidates were to submit their creative code related to any one of the given topic within a given time frame. They could submit code in the form of image or video.

    Candidates participated with enthusiasm and overwhelming entries were received.

    Candidates were judged on the basis of creativity in their codes.

    No. of participants:10

    Winner: Manpreet Kaur

    Prize: Barbeque Nation Voucher worth Rs.1000.

    DAY 3-

    We saw the same enthusiasm on day 3 as we observed on day 1. This day bought challenging codes on strings for coders. Two questions dealing with strings were asked from the candidates. Candidates were awarded marks on the basis of accuracy and speed of giving answers.

    No, of participants: 20

    Winner: Utsav Jain

    Prize: Barbeque Nation Voucher worth Rs.1000.

    Event -"DAILY QUIZOPHILLIA"

    This was the first successfully conducted event of coding club. Event was conducted from 9 July 2018 to 16 July 2018 in our whatsapp group. Problems based on various concepts were posted in the group regularly and candidates were asked to send their codes to the organizer.

    Regularly the candidates were ranked. Finally on 17 july 2018, based on daily evaluation of efficiency,accuracy,speed and consistency results were announced in three categories:-

    • Most quick responses
    • Consistent and Quickest
    • Efficient and correct codes

    Out of total around 30 participants 3 winners were announced as:-

  • Mr. Chintan Puri was awarded for most quick responses.
  • Ms.Umang Sharma has been the consistent and quickest coder of the contest.
  • Ms.Rasam Anand responded with most efficient and correct codes.
    •

    And so these three winners were awarded for their splendid performance with a Barbeque Nation voucher worth Rs.1000.

    ROADMAP

    We will be soon developing and widening our society so as to reach more and more number of students by involving them in a number of coding and related activities and events.

  • Widening the Scope:-

    • Our major focus at the beginning level is to focus on the students of Engineering Colleges in and around Punjab.After that we will try to expand our region beyond that and will be exploring pan India.

  • Mentorship Programme:-

    • Not only that with the mentorship program ,we will try to teach the tricks and techniques to the students with the help of which they will improve upon their coding abilities and help them get better opportunities.

  • Collaborations:-

    • We will be collaborating with many coding firms and help our students and students across the country to get the best of what they can.

  • Online and Offline Events:-

    • Our aim is to conduct both offline and online events related to programming that will help generate the competitive spirit among students and make them more task oriented and focussed.
